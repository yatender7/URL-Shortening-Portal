FAQ

Have a question?

What is a URL shortener?

A URL shortener is a service that takes a long URL and creates a shortened version, making it more manageable and convenient, especially for sharing on platforms with character limits.

How do I shorten a URL?

To shorten a URL, simply visit our website, paste the long URL into the provided space, and click the "Shorten" button. Your shortened URL will be generated instantly.

Is registration required to use the URL shortener?

No, registration is not required for basic URL shortening. However, creating an account offers additional features, such as tracking and managing your shortened URLs.

How long will my shortened URL be valid?

By default, shortened URLs have no expiration date. They will remain active as long as our service is operational and the URL does not violate our terms of service.

Do you track or store any personal information from users?

We prioritize user privacy. We do store analytics data, but it is anonymized, and we do not collect personal information without user consent. Refer to our privacy policy for more information.
